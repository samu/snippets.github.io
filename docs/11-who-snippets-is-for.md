# Who Snippets is for

Snippets is built for people who want powerful tools without giving up control, clarity, or long-term ownership of their data.

It is especially well suited for:

## Knowledge workers & thinkers

If you:

- think in systems, not just pages
- want to connect ideas over time
- value structure _and_ freedom

Snippets gives you references, attributes, dashboards, and rich text that scale with your thinking.

## Software engineers & technical users

Snippets is a great fit if you:

- work with complex projects and documentation
- want fast keyboard-driven workflows
- like keeping commands, notes, and knowledge close together

Offline-first storage, GitHub sync, structured data, and <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>K</kbd> are designed with technical workflows in mind.

## Learners & students

If you study continuously or long-term:

- flashcards and decks support repetition and retention
- progress is tracked and visualized
- notes and learning material live in the same system

Learning becomes part of your knowledge base, not a separate activity.

## People who care about data ownership

Snippets is a strong choice if you:

- want to work offline
- don’t want to depend on a single cloud provider
- care about exporting, syncing, or self-hosting your data

You choose where your data lives. The app adapts to that choice.
