# Snippets documentation

Snippets is an offline-first note-taking app built around structured data, flexible organization, and powerful editing — without locking you into a single way of thinking or working.

## Purposely not Markdown-based

Snippets is intentionally **not** built on Markdown.

Markdown works well for technical documentation and version-controlled content.  
For personal knowledge management, it quickly becomes a constraint.

By storing content as **rich, structured data**, Snippets enables:

- expressive layouts
- embedded and structured elements
- richer metadata
- long-term extensibility

To avoid vendor lock-in, Snippets provides a simple **export-to-Markdown** option.

## Comparisons

Snippets overlaps with other note-taking apps, but its priorities are different.

### Snippets vs Notion

Notion excels at collaboration and shared databases.

Snippets focuses on:

- offline-first usage
- local and GitHub-based storage
- structured data without forcing everything into databases
- long-term personal knowledge systems

Notion is a collaborative workspace.  
Snippets is a personal knowledge system.

### Snippets vs Obsidian

Obsidian is centered around Markdown files and plugins.

Snippets differs by:

- using rich, structured data instead of plain text
- offering predictable built-in behavior
- treating tables, attributes, and dashboards as first-class features

Obsidian is file-centric.  
Snippets is structure-centric.

### Snippets vs Apple Notes

Apple Notes prioritizes simplicity and OS integration.

Snippets is designed for:

- complex documents
- structured organization
- long-term knowledge growth
- cross-platform usage

Apple Notes is great for quick notes.  
Snippets is built for depth.

### Snippets vs Evernote

Evernote focuses on collecting and searching information.

Snippets emphasizes:

- local-first usage
- explicit structure
- flexible organization beyond notebooks
- user-controlled sync options

Evernote is a digital filing cabinet.  
Snippets is a composable system.

### Snippets vs UpNote

UpNote offers a clean, fast writing experience.

Snippets goes further by:

- supporting structured data alongside writing
- offering dashboards, tables, and flashcards
- scaling from simple notes to complex systems

UpNote is a focused writing app.  
Snippets is a platform for thinking, learning, and organizing.
