# Design philosophy

Snippets is guided by a small set of clear design principles.  
These principles influence every feature and trade-off in the app.

## Lightweight

Snippets aims to stay mentally and technically lightweight.

- No unnecessary abstraction
- No forced workflows
- Features exist to support thinking, not to demand configuration

## Fast

Performance matters.

- Interactions should feel immediate
- Editing should never feel sluggish
- Large documents and knowledge bases should remain responsive

Speed is treated as a core feature, not an optimization.

## Local-first

Your data lives on your device first.

- The app works fully offline
- Sync is optional and user-controlled
- Network access is an enhancement, not a requirement

This makes Snippets resilient, private, and dependable.

## Simple (but not simplistic)

Snippets favors **simple concepts** over shallow ones.

- Few core ideas, composed in flexible ways
- Predictable behavior over hidden magic
- Power emerges from combination, not from complexity

Snippets is designed to grow with you — without becoming heavier over time.
